Getting started

How to Play

01
Move your explorer
Use W A S D or the ↑ ↓ ← → arrow keys to walk around. Walk directly over glowing bobbing items to collect them — no button needed.
02
Craft a portal key
Open the ⚗️ Craft tab on the right panel. Each world has a unique portal key recipe. Collect enough resources, hit Craft Portal Key, then press Space to leap to the next dimension.
03
Light the dark worlds
Some worlds are pitch dark or wrapped in fog on arrival. Check your 🎒 Bag — if you've crafted a lantern or lens before, hit Use ✨ to clear the effect instantly. Otherwise craft one first.
04
Customise your character
Click 🧝 Character in the top bar. Choose from 20 outfit styles, 15 hair styles, 23 hair colours, coloured irises, 21 accessories, and trim colours. Your look saves automatically.
05
Revisit any world, anytime
Click 🌍 Worlds to open the dimension switcher. Every world you've discovered is listed — tap Travel → to hop back without spending a portal key.
06
Keep exploring
After dimension 8, the worlds never end. Procedural hybrids blend two base worlds together — new names, new palettes, new resources, new music. Some will have darkness or fog too. How far can you go?
